# YouTube Polls

Goal: learn about a YouTube audience's interests, challenges, preferences, and content needs. Each poll should improve general audience understanding.

## Rules

- One clear question + exactly four options.
- Each poll stands alone. Never assume voters saw or voted in any other poll — no follow-ups that depend on prior polls.
- Options must be very clear, 3–6 words max.
- Each poll is its own independent research question. Do not design a poll as a follow-up to, or a drill-down into, a previous poll's result. Treat every poll as if it's the first and only thing asked of the audience. Generate questions from scratch against the research goals — not from what was last asked.
